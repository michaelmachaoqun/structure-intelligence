# Structure Intelligence — Research Notes and Tools

This repository serves as a structured container for ongoing research on 
intelligence, structure, system behavior, and their underlying physical and 
computational principles.

The content is organized into several layers to ensure clarity, safety, and 
progressive development. Most materials are work-in-progress and will evolve 
as the research continues.
