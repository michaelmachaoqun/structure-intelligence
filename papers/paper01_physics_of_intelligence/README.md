# Toward a Physics of Intelligence  
## A Unified Framework of Mass, Energy, and Intelligence

This folder contains the Chinese and English versions of the paper.

### Files
- **paper_cn.pdf** — 中文版  
- **paper_en.pdf** — English version  

### Description
This paper proposes a unified physical framework linking mass, energy, and intelligence.  
It introduces:
- The energy–intelligence equation  
- The structural origin of intelligent behavior  
- The physical interpretation of intelligence as constrained energy unfolding  
- A unified view across physical, biological, and artificial systems  

### Status
Part of the Structure Intelligence research program.
