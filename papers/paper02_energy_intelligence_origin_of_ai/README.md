# Carbon-Based Internalization → Civilization Externalization → Silicon-Based Integration  
## The Physical Origin of AI from the Energy–Intelligence Equation Perspective

This folder contains the Chinese and English versions of the paper.

### Files
- **paper_cn.pdf** — 中文版  
- **paper_en.pdf** — English version  

### Description
This paper traces the physical origin of intelligence and AI through three evolutionary stages:
1. Carbon-based internalization  
2. Civilization-level externalization  
3. Silicon-based integration  

It interprets AI as a continuation of energy–intelligence structural evolution.
